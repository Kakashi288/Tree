Family Timeline

1222 - 1400s - Ancient Origins

The Foundation

The Bhalsod family lineage traces back to the founder Ramshi, who established our ancestral roots in north-western India during this era. His descendants‚ÄîRandhir, Palanshi, Lunshi, Jethshi, and Kika‚Äîsettled in the region between Gujarat and Rajasthan, laying the foundation for generations to come.

1500s - 1700s - Medieval Era

Growth & Expansion

Following generations dispersed across India, driven by the search for fertile lands, expanding trade routes, and opportunities in emerging commercial centers. During this transformative period of Mughal expansion and regional development, family members established themselves as merchants and traders. They built strong commercial networks, engaged in textile trade, and contributed to the flourishing marketplaces of their adopted regions, adapting to new communities while preserving their ancestral heritage.

1800s - 1900s - Modern Foundation

Building Legacy

As generations evolved, family members embarked on bold journeys beyond India's borders, migrating to Africa and the Western world in pursuit of new opportunities. Despite settling in distant lands, they remained deeply connected to their roots, keeping cultural and ancestral traditions alive through language, festivals, and customs. This era marked the beginning of a global diaspora, as the Bhalsod family established thriving communities abroad while honoring the heritage passed down from their forebears.

1950s - 2000s - Contemporary Era

Transformation & Progress

Further generations established themselves in America and the United Kingdom, embracing the opportunities of the post-war era. With modernization transforming the labor market, family members pursued diverse professions‚Äîfrom engineering and medicine to entrepreneurship and technology. 2000s - 2026 - Present Day

Today & Tomorrow

Family members across the globe continue to grow personally and professionally in the age of data, artificial intelligence, and unprecedented technological advancement. Spread across continents yet connected through digital innovation, the current generation faces both immense opportunities and unique challenges.
